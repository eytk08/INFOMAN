About

Welcome to MediVault, a centralized database system for medical histories. Our structured system is meticulously designed to store and manage patients’ health information. We focus on personal details, previous diagnoses, medications, allergies, and surgeries, if applicable. Our aim is to provide a comprehensive view of a patient’s medical history, facilitating informed decision-making, personalized treatment plans, medication choices, and care strategies.

This website is a final requirement for the Information Management course. It is a collaborative effort by the 2nd Year Section 1N Group 2 Computer Science students from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines-Manila Campus, College of Computer Science, for the academic year 2024 - 2025.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Users fill out a form with their information.
  2. Upon submission, the form data is securely transmitted to a server.
  3. The server processes and validates the data, ensuring it meets requirements.
  4. The validated data is then securely stored in a database using database management systems MySQL.
  5. This ensures that user information is safely kept and can be accessed securely when needed.
